Exploding Popcorn

Description

While their parents are out, Pingu and Pinga decide to cook some popcorn.
Pingu puts a pan on the warm stove and Pinga brings him a cob of corn.
Pingu puts the corn into the pan and they start jumping up and down until he puts the lid on.
They soon have a full pan of popcorn which they both gobble up straight away.
Pinga wants to make more popcorn so she puts wood into the boiler.
Pingu loads all the corn he can, and soon popcorn starts exploding out of the boiler, all over the floor...

Ingredients

  • no oil whatsoever
  • no seansoning of any kind
  • as many popcorn kernels to fill up the entire pot

Steps

  1. fill up the entire pot with popcorn kernels
  2. wait for pot to explode with popcorn
  3. fill up some toy boxes with said popcorn
  4. sledge around the neighborhood and deliver some popcorn to everyone
