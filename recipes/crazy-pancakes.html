Crazy Pancakes

Description

Mother is making pancakes and Pingu and Pinga want to help.
When the mixture is ready, Mother pours it onto the frying pan.
Pingu and Pinga want to try and flip the pancake, and Mother shows them how.
Way too excited, Pingu flips the pancakes all over the igloo...
