Birthday Cake

Description

One day, Pingu walks past a bakery with a large cake on display.
The baker shows Pingu his secret recipe and presents Pingu with a baker's hat.
Pingu thanks the baker and rushes home to do some baking himself!
Pingu puts way too much yeast into the baking mixure and then...

Ingredients

  • no butter
  • way too much yeast
  • as much flour as you wish
  • maybe some milk
  • probably no eggs

Steps

  1. find a bowl to mix the ingredients in
  2. tell your sister to whisk the baking mixture
  3. forget how much yeast you need to put in, so pour the whole thing in
  4. bake it in the oven
  5. watch the stove start to rattle and the oven door bulge
  6. takes immediate cover in the toy box
  7. have mother and father walk in just as a giant loaf of bread jumps out of the oven
  8. enjoy the sensational aroma
  9. dig in!
