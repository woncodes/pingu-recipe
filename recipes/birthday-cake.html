Birthday Cake

Description

One day, Pingu walks past a bakery with a large cake on display.
The baker shows Pingu his secret recipe and presents Pingu with a baker's hat.
Pingu thanks the baker and rushes home to do some baking himself!
Pingu puts way too much yeast into the baking mixure and then...

Ingredients

  • no butter
  • way too much yeast
  • all-purpose flour
  • some milk
  • probably no eggs

Steps

  1. Pingu finds a bowl to mix the ingredients in
  2. Pinga helps her brother whisk the baking mixture
  3. Pingu forgets how much yeast he needs to put in, so he pours a lot in
  4. Pingu pours the mixture on a baking tray and bakes it in the oven
  5. The stove starts rattling and the oven door starts bulging
  6. Pingu and Pinga takes cover in the toy box
  7. Mother and Father arrive home just as a giant loaf of bread jumps out of the oven
  8. They all go over to inspect it and the aroma is sensational
